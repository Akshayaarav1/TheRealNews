Stress

What is stress?

According to the WHO, stress can be defined as any type of change that causes physical, emotional or psychological strain. Stress is your body's response to anything that requires attention or action. Everyone experiences stress to some degree. The way you respond to stress, however, make a big difference to your overall well-being.

Why do people stress?

People often stress because of not knowing what to do, what will happen, or because they are just sad. Stress is mostly found in students because of studies, exams and many other responsibilities at the same time.
